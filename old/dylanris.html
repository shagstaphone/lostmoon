DYLAN RIS

Music Director / Composer / Writer / Guitar

As a comedian, Dylan has written extensively for The Daily Show’s Indecision series, Comedy Central, Politics Daily, and AOL. As a musician, he has composed the original score for twenty-four plays and films, including the forthcoming thriller Trail of Blood, produced by Joe Dante.  His music has been heard on PBS television, in the Dave Eggers-curated Wholphin, at Lincoln Center’s American Living Room Series, and in film festivals a’plenty. A reformed academic, Dylan holds degrees from Berklee College of Music and Northwestern University.
