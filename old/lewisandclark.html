Additional Reading

Thomas Jefferson, who conceived the Corps of Discovery, once wrote: "[W]ere it left to me to decide whether we should have a government without newspapers, or newspapers without a government, I should not hesitate a moment to prefer the latter."

What better way to honor our third President's love of journalism than to read the Lewis & Clark Press Release?
