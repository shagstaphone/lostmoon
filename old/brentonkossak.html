BRENTON KOSSAK

Bass Guitar


After studying music at California State University Northridge, Brenton has been playing upright and electric bass around Los Angeles while also working as a music composer, arranger, teacher, and video game tester. When he's not playing with the Moon Units, his hobbies include phat-groove-laying, cooking, and running bare-chested through the mountains.
